# Grant Submission Guide for BountyBucks

## Priority Grant Applications

### **1. Solana Foundation Grants** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Deadline**: Rolling applications  
**Amount**: $25,000 - $500,000  
**Success Rate**: 15-20%  

#### **Application Process:**
1. **Visit**: https://solana.org/ecosystem/grants
2. **Create Account**: Use your GitHub account
3. **Submit Application**: Use our prepared grant application
4. **Follow-up**: Email grants@solana.org after submission

#### **Key Requirements:**
- ✅ Solana-based project
- ✅ Open source code
- ✅ Clear use case
- ✅ Strong team
- ✅ Community impact

#### **Tips for Success:**
- Emphasize Solana ecosystem contribution
- Highlight technical innovation
- Show community engagement
- Provide clear milestones

---

### **2. Gitcoin Grants** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Deadline**: Every 2 weeks  
**Amount**: $5,000 - $50,000 per round  
**Success Rate**: 30-40%  

#### **Application Process:**
1. **Visit**: https://grants.gitcoin.co/
2. **Create Profile**: Complete your profile
3. **Submit Project**: Use our grant application
4. **Community Voting**: 2-week voting period

#### **Key Requirements:**
- ✅ Open source project
- ✅ Public good contribution
- ✅ Active community
- ✅ Clear funding use

#### **Tips for Success:**
- Focus on public good aspects
- Engage with community during voting
- Provide regular updates
- Show impact metrics

## Common Rejection Reasons & Solutions

### **1. "Not enough technical innovation"**
**Solution**: Emphasize hybrid token model and integrity system

### **2. "Team too small"**
**Solution**: Highlight individual expertise and advisor network

### **3. "Market too competitive"**
**Solution**: Show unique differentiation and first-mover advantage

### **4. "Unclear use of funds"**
**Solution**: Provide detailed budget breakdown and milestones

### **5. "Weak community"**
**Solution**: Build community before applying, show engagement metrics

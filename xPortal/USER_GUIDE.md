## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit portal.ask** in your web browser
2. **Click "Sign Up"** in the top navigation
3. **Fill in your details**:
   - Email address (required)
   - Username (unique, required)
   - Password (strong password recommended)
4. **Verify your email** (if required)
5. **Complete your profile** (optional but recommended)

### Setting Up Your Profile

#### Profile Picture
- Click on your profile picture in the top right
- Select "Edit Profile"
- Upload a profile picture (supports JPG, PNG, GIF)
- Recommended size: 400x400 pixels

#### Profile Information
- **Bio**: Tell the community about yourself
- **Location**: Where you're based (optional)
- **Website**: Your personal website or portfolio
- **Social Media**: Connect your accounts

#### Social Media Links
You can add links to:
- GitHub
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- Instagram
- Facebook
- YouTube
- TikTok
- Discord
- Reddit
- Medium
- Stack Overflow
- Dev.to

### First Steps

1. **Explore the Community**
   - Browse the main feed to see what others are posting
   - Read through some questions and answers
   - Get familiar with the platform's style

2. **Vote on Content**
   - Use the upvote/downvote buttons on posts and answers
   - This helps you understand what the community values

3. **Create Your First Post**
   - Start with a simple question or share something you've learned
   - Don't worry about making it perfect - the community will help improve it

## Creating Content

### Writing Posts (Questions)

#### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Click "Create Post"** in the navigation
2. **Write a clear title** that describes your question
3. **Add detailed content** explaining what you need help with
4. **Include code examples** if relevant (use the code block editor)
5. **Add media** if it helps explain your question
6. **Add tags** to help others find your post
7. **Optionally add a bounty** to incentivize answers
8. **Review and publish**

#### Writing Good Questions
- **Be specific**: Instead of "How do I code?", ask "How do I create a React component that fetches data from an API?"
- **Show your work**: Include what you've tried so far
- **Provide context**: Explain your situation and goals
- **Use clear language**: Write in a way that others can understand

#### Adding Code Blocks
1. Click the code block button in the editor
2. Select your programming language
3. Paste your code
4. The code will be syntax-highlighted automatically

#### Adding Media
- **Images**: Screenshots, diagrams, or photos
- **Videos**: Screen recordings or demonstrations
- **File size limit**: 10MB per file
- **Supported formats**: JPG, PNG, GIF, MP4, WebM

### Providing Answers

#### How to Answer
1. **Read the question carefully** and understand what's being asked
2. **Click "Add Answer"** below the question
3. **Write a comprehensive response** that addresses all parts of the question
4. **Include code examples** if relevant
5. **Explain your reasoning** so others can learn
6. **Submit your answer**

#### Writing Good Answers
- **Be thorough**: Address all aspects of the question
- **Provide examples**: Show, don't just tell
- **Explain your approach**: Help others understand your thinking
- **Be respectful**: Even if you disagree with the question
- **Cite sources**: If you're referencing external information

### Adding Comments

#### When to Comment
- **Ask for clarification** on a question or answer
- **Provide additional information** or resources
- **Share your experience** with similar problems
- **Thank someone** for their help
- **Start a discussion** about the topic

#### Comment Guidelines
- Keep comments relevant to the post
- Be respectful and constructive
- Avoid spam or self-promotion
- Use comments for discussion, not for new questions

## Bounty System

### What are Bounties?

Bounties are rewards offered for accepted answers to questions. They're a way to incentivize high-quality responses and show appreciation for helpful community members.

### Creating a Bounty

#### When to Add a Bounty
- **Urgent questions** that need quick answers
- **Complex problems** that require significant effort
- **Specialized topics** where experts are needed
- **Showing appreciation** for the community

#### How to Create a Bounty
1. **Create your post** as usual
2. **Click "Add Bounty"** before publishing
3. **Enter the amount** in PORTAL tokens
4. **Set an expiration date** (optional)
5. **Confirm the bounty**

#### Bounty Best Practices
- **Set reasonable amounts**: Consider the complexity of your question
- **Be clear about requirements**: Specify what constitutes an acceptable answer
- **Engage with answers**: Provide feedback and ask for clarification
- **Accept answers promptly**: Don't leave bounties hanging

### Claiming Bounties

#### How Bounties Work
1. **You post a question** with a bounty
2. **Community members provide answers**
3. **You review and accept the best answer**
4. **The bounty is automatically paid** to the answer author
5. **The answer is marked as accepted**

#### Accepting an Answer
- **Review all answers** before making a decision
- **Consider the quality** and completeness of the response
- **Ask for clarification** if needed
- **Accept the best answer** by clicking "Accept Answer & Claim Bounty"

#### Bounty Expiration
- Bounties can have expiration dates
- If no answer is accepted before expiration, the bounty is refunded
- You can extend the expiration date if needed

## Reputation & Integrity

### Understanding Reputation

Your reputation score reflects your contributions to the community. It's earned through helpful actions and quality content.

#### How to Earn Reputation Points
- **Create a post**: +10 points
- **Provide an answer**: +5 points
- **Have your answer accepted**: +15 points
- **Receive upvotes**: +2 points per upvote
- **Receive quality upvotes**: +5 points per quality upvote
- **Receive downvotes**: -1 point per downvote

#### Reputation Levels
- **Beginner**: 0-99 points
- **Contributor**: 100-499 points
- **Expert**: 500-999 points
- **Master**: 1000+ points

### Integrity System

The integrity system allows community members to rate each other based on their behavior and contributions.

#### Rating Contexts
- **Bounty Rejection**: When someone rejects a valid answer to their bounty
- **Answer Quality**: Quality of answers provided
- **Communication**: How well someone communicates
- **Spam**: Posting irrelevant or promotional content
- **Harassment**: Inappropriate or harmful behavior
- **General Behavior**: Overall conduct in the community

#### How to Rate Someone
1. **Click the integrity rating button** (shield icon) on their content
2. **Select the context** for your rating
3. **Choose a rating** from 1-10
4. **Provide a reason** for your rating
5. **Submit the rating**

#### Rating Guidelines
- **Be fair and objective**: Rate based on behavior, not personal feelings
- **Provide constructive feedback**: Help others improve
- **Use appropriate contexts**: Choose the most relevant rating category
- **Avoid abuse**: Don't use ratings to harass others

## Wallet Management

### Understanding Your Virtual Wallet

Your virtual wallet manages your PORTAL tokens for bounties and rewards. It's separate from your Solana wallet and is used only within the platform.

### Depositing Funds

#### Direct Deposit (Recommended)
1. **Go to your wallet page**
2. **Click "Deposit"**
3. **Choose "Direct Deposit"**
4. **Connect your Solana wallet** (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
5. **Enter the amount** you want to deposit
6. **Confirm the transaction** in your wallet
7. **Wait for confirmation**

#### Manual Deposit
1. **Go to your wallet page**
2. **Click "Deposit"**
3. **Choose "Manual Deposit"**
4. **Copy the deposit address**
5. **Send SOL from your wallet** to that address
6. **Enter the transaction signature**
7. **Submit for verification**

### Withdrawing Funds

1. **Go to your wallet page**
2. **Click "Withdraw"**
3. **Enter the amount** you want to withdraw
4. **Enter your Solana wallet address**
5. **Confirm the withdrawal**
6. **Wait for processing**

### Transaction History

Your wallet page shows:
- **Current balance**
- **Total deposited**
- **Total withdrawn**
- **Total earned** from bounties
- **Total spent** on bounties
- **Transaction history** with details

### Wallet Security

- **Never share your private keys**
- **Use a secure wallet** (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
- **Verify transaction details** before confirming
- **Keep your wallet software updated**

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Log In
- **Check your email and password**
- **Try resetting your password**
- **Clear your browser cache**
- **Try a different browser**

#### Can't Upload Media
- **Check file size** (max 10MB)
- **Verify file format** (JPG, PNG, GIF, MP4, WebM)
- **Check your internet connection**
- **Try refreshing the page**

#### Bounty Not Working
- **Check your wallet balance**
- **Verify the bounty amount**
- **Ensure you're on the correct network**
- **Contact support** if the issue persists

#### Can't Vote
- **Make sure you're logged in**
- **Check if you've already voted**
- **Try refreshing the page**
- **Clear your browser cache**
